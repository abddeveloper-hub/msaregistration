Privacy Policy

We value the privacy and trust of every visitor and user of this portal. This policy explains how personal information is collected, used, stored, and protected.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, academic records, and account credentials when you register or sign in.

How We Use It

  • To create and manage your account
  • To support admissions, attendance, and academic administration
  • To improve the portal experience and ensure secure access

Data Security

We use secure authentication and cloud storage measures to protect user data. However, no system is completely immune to risk, and users should also protect their own login credentials.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact the administration at portal@msaukkuda.org.
